_Update: Development team, 05-Aug-2024_

To manage the "About SciELO" link, the access to the website administrative area is needed.

The _link_ to access the administrative area is [http://yourdomain/admin](http://yourdomain/admin)

After authenticating in the administrative area, you can view a menu as follows:

![Screenshot 2024-08-05 at 15 30 56](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c095305c-cd99-4cf1-9648-1fecb768508f)

By clicking "Pages", a list of available pages will be displayed, among which are those that make up the "About SciELO" section. These pages are identified by the absence of the journal acronym, meaning that pages belonging to "About SciELO" do not show the journal acronym identifier.

Example of a page that is part of "About SciELO":

![Screenshot 2024-08-05 at 15 34 41](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/943b8639-24af-4aec-a838-cea6fda61db6)

Note that there is only one page without the journal acronym, therefore it belongs to "About SciELO", while the others are pages for journals that are part of "About the journal".

In this area, you can add new pages by clicking "Create":
